Photography is a form of expression that combines art and technique to capture moments, emotions, and details of the world around us. Through a camera - whether professional or even just a phone - it is possible to freeze time in an image, conveying feelings without the need for words. As an art, photography allows the creator to show their vision of the world, playing with light, color, shapes, and emotions.

Each photograph can tell a story, evoke memories, or invite reflection. As a technical skill, it involves knowledge about how to use the camera, focusing, exposure, framing, and other elements that help create well - composed images. Learning these technical aspects is essential to use the camera as a creative tool rather than just a device that records what it sees. Today, photography is a part of our everyday lives - from family photos to professional art, from social media to advertising campaigns. The beauty is that you don't need to be an expert to get started; all it takes is a curious eye, careful observation, and the courage to capture what inspires you.
